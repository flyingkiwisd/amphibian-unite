# Amphibian Unite — Get Started

Hey team,

We built something I'm excited to share: **Amphibian Unite** — our internal operating system that puts everything about how we run as a team in one place.

**Repo:** https://github.com/flyingkiwisd/amphibian-unite

---

## Quick Start (2 minutes)

```bash
git clone https://github.com/flyingkiwisd/amphibian-unite.git
cd amphibian-unite
npm install
npm run dev
```

Open **http://localhost:3000** — pick your name on the login screen and you're in.

---

## What You'll Find

- **Your Operating System** — click your card in Team view to see your 360 feedback, personal OS, quality scores, RDS framework, risk framework, and journal
- **Journal** — Morning Start + End of Day prompts to ground your day, plus Weekly and Monthly planning templates
- **Agent Intel** — your assigned AI agents surface strategic insights, hard questions, and research prompts tailored to your role
- **Leaderboard** — points system tracking quality scores, RDS progress, risk coverage
- **Tasks** — Kanban board with drag-and-drop, create/edit/delete
- **OKRs, Roadmap, AI Edge, Decision Log, Notes, Activity** — all live

Use **Cmd+K** to search across everything.
