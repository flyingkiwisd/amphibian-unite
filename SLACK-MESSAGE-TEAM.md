# Slack Message — Team Launch of Amphibian Unite

---

Hey team :wave:

We built something I think changes how we operate — **Amphibian Unite: Our 10x Internal Operating System.**

Instead of our strategy, roles, feedback, and goals living scattered across docs and spreadsheets, it all lives in one place now — alive, interactive, and built around *you*.

**Here's what's in it for you:**

:dart: **Your Operating System** — your role, your 360 feedback, your qualities scorecard, your RDS framework, your risk framework — all in one view. Click your card and see exactly what you own, where you're growing, and what to focus on.

:brain: **AI Agents thinking for you** — 14 agents assigned across the team, proactively surfacing strategic insights, hard questions you should be asking, and research you didn't know you needed. This isn't a chatbot — it's an intelligence layer built around your seat.

:notebook_with_decorative_cover: **Journals that sharpen your day** — Morning Start, End of Day, Weekly Plan, Monthly Plan. Guided prompts designed to help you think better, not just check boxes.

:trophy: **Leaderboard** — a points system that rewards delivery: quality scores, RDS progress, risk coverage, journal streaks. Transparent, gamified, tied to real outcomes.

:rocket: **Plus:** OKRs, Tasks (Kanban drag-and-drop), Roadmap, AI Edge Assessment, Decision Log, Notes, Activity Feed, Cmd+K search across everything — all mobile-responsive.

**This is Crawl phase.** Walk phase connects real data (Slack, Google Drive, database persistence). Run phase makes the agents think for us daily — proactive digests, predictive risk scoring, automated insights. Your feedback right now determines what we build next.

---

**How to try it (2 minutes):**

```
git clone https://github.com/flyingkiwisd/amphibian-unite.git
cd amphibian-unite
npm install
npm run dev
```
Open **http://localhost:3000** — pick your name and you're in.
