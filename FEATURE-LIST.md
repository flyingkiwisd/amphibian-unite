# Amphibian Unite: 10x Internal Operating System — Feature List

*Everything this app does. Share with the team to refine, prioritize, and expand.*

## 1. Dashboard (Home)

- **Company-wide KPI summary** — AUM, revenue, edge rating, team size at a glance
- **Quick-nav cards** to all views
- **Status indicators** for agents, OKRs, tasks, and team health
- **Real-time pulse** of what matters most today

---

## 2. Team Operating Systems

### Landing Grid
- **Team member cards** showing name, role, status (Active/Hiring), one-sentence role description
- **Quick stats** on each card: qualities at 4+, RDS items in progress, risk items
- Click any member to open their full Operating System

### Per-Member Detail (8 tabs):

#### Tab 1: Overview
- Personal 1-pager: avatar, name, role, mantra
- Single-threaded ownership (what this person — and only this person — owns)
- KPIs with clear targets
- Non-negotiables (hard lines)
- **Agent assignments** — which AI agents this person is primary owner of and which they support

#### Tab 2: 360 Feedback
- **Wins for Team** — what this person brings to the org (green)
- **Makes Harder** — what creates drag for others (amber)
- **Start Doing** — actions to begin (blue)
- **Stop Doing** — behaviors to eliminate (red)
- **Support Needed** — what they need from the org
- **Role Clarity** — where boundaries are unclear
- **High Leverage Moves** — actions with outsized impact

#### Tab 3: Operating System
- **Seat / Not This Seat** — clear lane definition
- **Morning Checklist** — daily startup ritual
- **Commitments** — personal standards
- **Decision Filter** — questions to ask before every call
- **Evening Reflection** — daily close-out
- **Weekly Pulse** — weekly check-in questions
- **Mantra** — personal operating principle (highlighted)

#### Tab 4: Qualities (Best-in-Class Self-Assessment)
- Summary bar: average score, total qualities, 4+ count, growth edges
- Each quality shows:
  - Name + description
  - Score bar (1-5, teal gradient)
  - What the 360 says about this quality
  - Weekly self-assessment question
- **Growth edges** highlighted in rose for qualities below 3

#### Tab 5: RDS Framework (Remove / Delegate / Systematize)
- Three-column layout: Remove, Delegate, Systematize
- Each item has status badge: Identified, In Progress, Done
- Delegate items show who it's delegated to
- Systematize items describe the system being built
- Summary stats: total, in progress, completed

#### Tab 6: Risk Framework
- **Personal Risks** table: risk, likelihood, impact, mitigation
- Color-coded badges (high = red, medium = amber, low = green)
- **Decision Principles** — questions to ask before key decisions
- **Red Lines** — actions this person must never take (rose highlights)
- Mobile-responsive: table on desktop, cards on mobile

#### Tab 7: Journal
- **Four journal modes** with tab switcher:
  1. **Morning Start** — regulation check, top 3 today, NOT doing list, proactive moves, vision alignment
  2. **End of Day** — wins, tension dump, cleanup queue, quality check, tomorrow prep
  3. **Weekly Plan** — weekly top 3, RDS review, fire management, blockers, role clarity, personal growth
  4. **Monthly Plan** — monthly reflection, RDS audit, qualities scoring, risk review, next month focus, becoming a better human
- In-session text areas for reflection (each prompt has its own entry field)
- Date stamp

#### Tab 8: Agent Intel (Proactive AI Thinking)
- **Your AI Agents** — which agents serve this person (owner vs. support)
- **Proactive Intelligence** feed with three types:
  - **Strategic Insights** — ideas and connections the agent surfaces proactively
  - **Key Questions** — hard questions the agent asks on your behalf (blind spots, assumptions)
  - **Research Prompts** — benchmarks, best practices, competitive intelligence to investigate
- Each insight shows the source agent
- Explanation of what your agents think about: strategic, questions, research

### PDF Export
- Download any tab as a PDF for sharing or printing

## 3. AI Agents (14 Agents)

- **Agent cards** with status (Active, Building, Planned), priority level
- Click to view full agent detail: capabilities, data sources, status
- Each agent mapped to a **primary owner** and **direct report**

### Agent Roster:
1. **North Star Agent** — Vision, mission, values, strategic alignment
2. **Identity & Roles Agent** — Personal operating systems, decision rights, role clarity
3. **OKR & KPI Engine** — Objectives, key results, weekly top 3
4. **Task Commander** — Project management, Kanban, deadlines, blockers
5. **Slack Signal Intelligence** — Extract decisions and action items from Slack
6. **Portfolio Intelligence** — Fund performance, risk metrics, manager data
7. **LP & Investor Trust** — Investor communications, pipeline, trust signals
8. **Governance & Decision Agent** — Decision logs, compliance, governance
9. **Financial Intelligence** — Cash runway, budgets, revenue tracking
10. **Culture & Alignment** — Team health, values enforcement, founder alignment
11. **AI Edge Agent** — AI infrastructure progress, edge rating evolution
12. **Risk Monitor** — Risk limits, portfolio risk, regime classification
13. **Growth Agent** — Partnership pipeline, distribution, allocator outreach
14. **Ops Flow Agent** — Operational workflows, SLAs, process standards

## 11. Platform Features

### Command Palette (Cmd+K)
- Search across all views, team members, agents, tasks
- Quick navigation to any section
- Keyboard-first interface

### Mobile Bottom Navigation
- 4 primary tabs: Home, Tasks, Agents, OKRs
- "More" menu expands to: Team, Decisions, Roadmap, Notes, Activity, AI Edge
- Search accessible from More menu
- Safe area padding for notched phones

### PDF Export (All Views)
- One-click PNG/PDF export on Dashboard, OKRs, Tasks, Roadmap, AI Edge, Team
- Uses html2canvas for accurate rendering
- Toast notification during export

### Responsive Design
- Desktop: full sidebar with collapse toggle
- Mobile: bottom navigation bar, sidebar hidden
- All views adapt to screen size

### Authentication
- Login screen with team member selection
- Session-based (client-side)

### API Routes (8 endpoints)
- `/api/team` — team member data
- `/api/agents` — agent data
- `/api/okrs` — OKR data
- `/api/kpis` — KPI data
- `/api/tasks` — task data
- `/api/notes` — notes data
- `/api/decisions` — decision log data
- `/api/export` — export functionality

## 12. Leaderboard & Incentives System

- **Team leaderboard** ranking by points earned
- **Points system** based on:
  - Tasks completed on time
  - OKR key results delivered
  - Quality scores trending up
  - RDS items completed (removing, delegating, systematizing)
  - Agent Intel acted on
  - Journal streak (daily consistency)
  - 360 improvement (quarter over quarter)
- **Bonus tracker** — tied to measurable outcomes
- **Weekly MVP** recognition
- **Streak tracking** — consecutive weeks of journal completion, task delivery, quality improvement
- **Public recognition feed** — celebrate wins across the team

---

## 13. Decision Fatigue Reducer
- **Agent-powered decision support** per role — not just insights but recommendations with rationale
- Pre-digested decisions based on role, decision filter, and current priorities
- "Based on your seat and context, here's what I recommend and why"
- Reduces cognitive load by turning passive intelligence into active decision support

---

## 14. Accountability Loops
- Morning "Top 3 Today" commitments auto-pulled into End of Day review
- Hit rate tracking: what percentage of daily commitments were completed
- Weekly accountability summary showing trends
- Close-the-loop mechanism between morning intentions and evening reflection

---

## 15. Founder Alignment Score
- Weekly 3-question alignment survey between founders (James, Andrew, Mark)
- Scored and tracked over time to detect drift early
- Prevents misalignment before it becomes a crisis
- Visual trend showing alignment score over quarters

---

## 16. Meeting Intelligence
- Pre-meeting agent generates: agenda from open blockers + priorities, pre-read from recent decisions, suggested outcomes
- Post-meeting captures: decisions and action items automatically
- Meetings become productive by default with AI-generated prep

---

## 17. "What Changed" Daily Digest
- Morning intelligence briefing: what happened overnight
- Fund performance updates, Slack decisions, tasks completed by others, risks that moved
- Walk in informed instead of spending 30 minutes catching up
- Personalized per role — each person sees what's relevant to their seat

---

## 18. Peer Feedback Micro-Pulses
- Weekly 1-question peer pulse: "Rate [person]'s collaboration this week 1-5" with optional comment
- Trends over time more valuable than one-time quarterly 360 reviews
- Lightweight, anonymous, continuous feedback loop
- Surfaces patterns in collaboration quality

---

## 19. Cash Runway & Burn Visibility
- Financial Intelligence agent surfaces: current burn rate, runway in months, revenue vs. plan
- Every team member sees the financial reality — transparency changes behavior
- Break-even AUM tracking and revenue sensitivity modeling
- Cash forecast dashboard visible to leadership

---

## 20. LP Health Score
- Composite score per LP: communication frequency, satisfaction signals, AUM trend, risk of churn
- Proactive outreach triggers when scores drop
- LP Trust agent monitors and alerts
- Pipeline tracking from intro → meeting → commitment

---

## 21. Competitive Intelligence Feed
- Growth Agent monitors peer crypto funds: AUM changes, strategy shifts, hiring signals, product launches
- Weekly digest: "Here's what the market is doing that you should know about"
- Competitive positioning awareness without manual research

---

## 22. Knowledge Graph
- Connect decisions → OKRs they serve → people responsible → risks they mitigate
- Visual graph showing *why* things matter, not just *what* they are
- Linked views across the platform

---

## 23. Personal Energy Tracking
- 1-5 energy/focus rating each morning and evening in journal
- Over weeks, patterns emerge from calendar correlation
- Agent spots what you can't see: "You consistently rate energy low on Wednesdays — your calendar shows back-to-back meetings"

---

## 24. Role Drift Detection
- Compare what someone is *supposed* to own (seat description) vs what they're *actually* doing (tasks, decisions, meetings)
- Alert when there's significant drift
- Prevents people doing work that isn't theirs

---

## 25. Editable Sections (Real-Time Collaboration)
- **Inline editing** for Team OS sections — 360 feedback, operating system, qualities, RDS, risk framework
- **Editable roadmap** — team members can update their own tasks and milestones
- **Editable OKRs** — progress can be updated by owners in real-time
- **localStorage persistence** in Crawl phase, database persistence in Walk phase
- Each team member can update their own data without code changes
